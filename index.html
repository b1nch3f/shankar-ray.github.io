Introduction

Call center conversations are recorded all the time, they say its for training purposes. In reality this domain makes extensive use of different analytics technologies.

A simple google search would return several hundreds of results, the widespread use of call recordings to understand the demands of their customer. It's an interesting method to not only train the people joining customer support industry but also understanding the requirements of the customers.

Keeping this in mind, i found HP Idol OnDeman seems to be a good fit for performing sentiment analysis on the call records. The sentiment analysis API takes documents, texts as input and performs the sentiment analysis on that. It then returs the positive and negative sentiments. And also the aggregate sentiment and the score.

In my sample application, i'd be using a sample mp3 file hosted publicly and performing sentiment analysis on that.
